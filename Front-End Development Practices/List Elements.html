Zeenathul's Recipe for the Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

For the cookie dough:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter with the brown and white sugar until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients and stir until a soft dough forms.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips evenly.
  7. Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto a lined baking tray.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool for a few minutes before serving.
